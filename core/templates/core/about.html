About AI Study Planner

AI Study Planner is a study management tool built for students who want structure without the busywork. Instead of manually juggling subjects, deadlines, and exam dates, our rule-based AI recommendation engine looks at your priorities, subject difficulty, completion history, and upcoming exams to build a realistic daily schedule for you — automatically increasing focus time on subjects that need it most.

🎓 Built for students

Designed around how real students plan: subjects, tasks, exams, and goals.

🔍 Transparent AI

Every recommendation comes with a reason — no black-box scheduling.

📈 Upgradeable

Starts rule-based, designed to plug in a trained ML model later.
